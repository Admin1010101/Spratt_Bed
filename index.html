Why we built it

Spratt Labs is a small team with one stubborn idea.

Every time our dogs came back from a run or the yard, they brought the smell inside with them. The only ways to keep up were spraying them down with something every single time, or booking grooming so often it got expensive fast — and neither actually kept things fresh for long.

We wanted a way to control that smell continuously, quietly in the background — without spraying our dogs or living at the groomer — and to make the bed itself genuinely better while we were at it. That's what led us to build SprattOS™: sensing, airflow, purification, and pet-safe scent in one comfortable bed. It took a lot of burned-out prototypes, and the Spratt Bed is now heading into its first production run.

Backing the pre-launch puts you in the room for the part we care about most: getting the details right before we make thousands of them.

Good questions

The things people ask first.

Is my $5 reservation a charge for the bed?+

No. It's a small, fully refundable deposit that holds your founder pricing and your spot in the first run. You're only charged for a Spratt Bed when the Kickstarter campaign launches and you confirm your pledge.

Does the bed diffuse on its own when my dog lies down?+

No — and this is the part people most often get wrong. AirSense™ recognizes your dog, but every release is controlled by you in the Spratt App™. Run it on demand with manual boost, set a schedule, or pause it. The bed does not spray automatically just because your dog settles in.

Then what is AirSense™ for?+

AirSense™ recognizes your dog and confirms the bed is in use — intentionally separate from the diffusion you control. It's also the foundation for features we're building next, like weight monitoring and health insights, so the same hardware keeps getting more useful through SprattOS™ updates.

Are the AirMood™ scents safe for dogs, cats, and kids?+

Yes. SprattOS™ uses dry diffusion rather than wet sprays, and AirMood™ oils were developed and confirmed by chemical engineers to be safe for families and pets — including cats, which many household scents are not. You control intensity in the app, and AirPure™ can deodorize fragrance-free. The full ingredient breakdown is shared with pledgers.

When does the Spratt Bed ship?+

We're targeting first deliveries in early 2027 after the Kickstarter campaign closes and tooling is finalized. Founders ship first, and we'll post timeline updates the whole way.

What happens if the campaign doesn't fund?+

If we don't hit our goal, every reservation deposit is refunded in full — that's the point of keeping it refundable. You risk nothing by reserving early.
